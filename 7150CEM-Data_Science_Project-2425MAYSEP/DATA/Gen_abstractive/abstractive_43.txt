Scotland produced a stunning run chase to beat Namibia by five wickets in the T20 World Cup 2024. Returning to the Kensington Oval after their washout against England  they achieved a target of 156 with nine balls to spare. A 74 run partnership between Richie Berrington and Michael Leask ultimately  saw Scotland home. Namibia captain Gerhard Erasmus had earlier turned matters in his side s favour with the ball  taking 2 for 29.
