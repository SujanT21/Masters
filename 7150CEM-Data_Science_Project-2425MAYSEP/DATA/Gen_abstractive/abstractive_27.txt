England thrash Oman for 47 in the space of 80 balls at the Sir Vivian Richards Stadium in Antigua. They needed just 19 to hunt that total down to hoist their troublesome net run rate firmly into the black. Adil Rashid was the pick of an outstanding four prong attack with figures of 4 for 11 before a turbo charged chase capped by Jos Buttler s 24 not out from eight balls. Scotland will be left with the daunting task of beating the Group B leaders Australia if they hope to progress to the Super Eight.
