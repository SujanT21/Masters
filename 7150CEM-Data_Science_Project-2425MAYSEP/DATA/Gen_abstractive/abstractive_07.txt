England booked their place in the semi finals of the T20 World Cup 2024 with a commanding 10 wicket win over USA in their final Super Eight match. Using just 9 4 overs to chase down 116  thanks to Jos Buttler s 83 not out from 38 deliveries  the defending champions have boosted their net run rate  NRR  to 1 992 for good measure. Such a dominant win was set up by Chris Jordan who became the first England player to take a men s T20I hat trick.
