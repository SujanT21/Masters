South Africa beat Afghanistan by nine wickets in the first semi final of T20 World Cup 2024. Aiden Markram and Reeza Hendricks calmly navigated the small chase on an exceedingly tricky Tarouba pitch. They bowled beautifully  fielded well and weathered some difficult early overs while pouncing on the loose balls. As their reward  they have their first ever berth in a men s World Cup final.
